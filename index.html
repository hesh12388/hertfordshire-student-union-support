üîí Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

This app processes the following data for official University of Hertfordshire Egypt Student Union operations:

  • Student names and contact information (provided by the university student union)
  • Meeting attendance, performance, participation
  • Task assignments, information, and completion status
  • User-generated content such as proposals, meeting, tasks, interviews, and warnings

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for:

  • Managing student union operations and activities
  • Tracking meeting attendance and member participation
  • Facilitating task, proposal, interview, and warning management
  • Maintaining official student union records

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. Access is restricted to authorized student union members and university staff only.

Data Sharing

Your information is not shared with third parties outside of official university business. Data may be shared with university administration as required for student union governance.

Your Rights

For questions about your personal data or to request access to your information, please contact the University of Hertfordshire Egypt Student Union or email us at hertssu-app@outlook.com
